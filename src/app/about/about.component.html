About Me

"Find your voice and inspire others to find theirs."
-Stephen Covey

I am a digital advocate with a passion for building beautiful, responsive web products.

Currently I program responsive web applications for Cardinal Solutions Group, an IT consulting company. Solving unique problems, collaborating with others, and growing from the outcome of my work, are all reasons why I love doing what I do.

In my free time I enjoy creative hobbies (like drawing) and being a community service leader. Since 2015, I have been the Charlotte Chapter Leader of Girl Develop It - a non-profit dedicated to providing affordable, judgment-free opportunities for women interested in software engineering. Come be apart of our community.

Come June, I will also spend my free time as a teaching assistant for UNC Charlotte's Full Stack Developer Boot Camp. This six month program will cover the basics from HTML, JavaScript, Node.js, and more. If you're curious about this program, or coding boot camps in general, please reach out to me to see if it's the right fit for you.

Despite web development being such a fundamental skill behind everything we do online, it is also a wonderful viewpoint from which to see the world - and I aim to make that accessible for others.
