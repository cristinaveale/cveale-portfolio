About Me

"Find your voice and inspire others to find theirs."
-Stephen Covey

I am a digital advocate with a passion for building beautiful, responsive web products.

I've been building web applications with Cardinal Solutions for the past three years. Solving unique problems, collaborating with others, and growing from the outcome of my work, are all reasons why I love doing what I do.

Despite web development being such a fundamental skill behind everything we do online, it is also a wonderful viewpoint from which to see the world - and I aim to make that accessible for others.

In my free time I enjoy creative hobbies, hanging out with my loved ones, and being a community service leader. Since 2015, I have been the Charlotte chapter leader of Girl Develop It, a non-profit dedicated to providing affordable, judgment-free opportunities for women interested in software engineering. Come be apart of our community.
